# Product Overview: SIDM (Sistema Integrado de Dados Municipais)

**Lane**: Product / Documentation (API Documentation & Developer Infrastructure)

## Audience
Devs, researchers, data analysts, civic tech, data journalists. Highly technical people who value reliability, accuracy, and clear documentation.

## Archetype
**The Sage (O Sábio)**
Truth, precision, reliable source. 
Secondary: The Architect (builder of infrastructure).

## Tone & Voice
Clear, direct, and authoritative without being bureaucratic. We avoid generic corporate clichés (like "synergy" or people shaking hands). We provide infrastructure and facts. We are the trusted source. 

## Positioning
The reliable source for municipal data: dispersed out there, integrated and ready to consume here.

## Brand Anti-References (What we avoid)
1. We are NOT a generic modern dev-tool (avoid the standard Stripe/Linear clone look with purple gradients and heavy pure-black dark modes).
2. We are NOT a bureaucratic government portal (avoid generic "government blue" and outdated institutional layouts).
